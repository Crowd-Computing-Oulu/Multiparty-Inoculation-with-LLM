Welcome to MindFort!

MindFort is designed to help you recognize and counter misinformation. Strengthen your critical thinking and expand your knowledge through engaging conversation.

When you click the "Get Started" button, you will enter a simulated conversation between a conversant and two bots. One of them is Marty, a bot that spreads misinformation. The other is Forty, The other is Forty that adopts one of three possible roles:

  • Supportive: Gently corrects misinformation and encourages trust in evidence-based information.
  • Refutational: Clearly and directly debunks false claims using facts, logic, and evidence.
  • Prebunking: Identifies misinformation techniques in advance and during the conversation. Also explains why misinformation is misleading or false.


Your task is to follow the conversation closely. At the end, you will be asked to identify which Forty's role and reflect on the conversation.
