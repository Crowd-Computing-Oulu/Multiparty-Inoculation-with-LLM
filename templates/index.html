Welcome to MindFort!

You will be shown a simulated conversation between a conversant and two bots (Marty and Forty). Marty spreads misinformation. Your task is to pay attention to Forty, and identify which of the three possible roles it plays.

Read the role explanation and example conversation below before you start.

Supportive

Agrees with the Conversant’s view and encourages trust in reliable information.

Refutational

Corrects Marty's false claims using clear facts, evidence, and logical explanations.

Prebunking

Preemptively warns and educates the Conversant about potential misleading claims and misinformation techniques(Emotional Language, Conspiracy Narratives, etc.)

When you are ready, click the button below to begin the conversation. At the end, you will answer which role Forty played and reflect briefly on the conversation.
