Welcome to MindFort!

MindFort is designed to help you recognize and counter misinformation. Strengthen your critical thinking and expand your knowledge through engaging conversation.

When you click the "Get Started" button below, you will enter a simulated conversation between a conversant and two bots. One of them is Marty, a bot that spreads misinformation. The other is Forty, that adopts one of three possible roles below:

Supportive: Supports the conversantâ€™s original opinion and encourages them to trust reliable information.
Refutational: Responds to false claims by correcting them using clear facts, evidence and logical explanations.
Prebunking: Warns and educates the conversant before misinformation appears, identifies the misinformation technique used by Marty, and explains why the claim is misleading or false.


Your task is to follow the conversation closely. At the end, you will be asked to identify Forty's role and reflect on the conversation.
